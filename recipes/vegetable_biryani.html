Recipe Vegetable Biryani

Pic Credits: Nish1892

Description

Vegetable biryani is an aromatic and flavorful Indian rice dish made by layering long-grain basmati rice with a spiced vegetable medley. Unlike a pulao, where ingredients are cooked together, a traditional Vegetable Dum Biryani uses the "dum" method—slow-cooking in a sealed pot to infuse the rice with deep, complex flavors

Ingredients

  • Rice: 1 cup basmati rice, soaked for 30 mins
  • vegetables: 1.5 cups mix (chopped carrots, beans, peas, cauliflower, potato).
  • Aromatics: 1 large onion (thinly sliced), 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 1-2 green chillies.
  • Spices: 1 tbsp cumin seeds, 1 bay leaf, 2 cardamom pods, 2 cloves, 1-inch cinnamon, 1 tbsp biryani masala, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp red chilli powder.
  • Liquids & Others: 2 tbsp ghee/oil. 1/4 cup yougurt (whisked), 1.5 to 2 cups water, mint & coriander leaves, salt.

Steps to Cook

  1. Prep Rice: Wash and soak the basmati rice for at least 30 minutes. Drain completely.
  2. Saute Aromatics: Heat ghee/oil in a thick-bottomed pot. Add whole spices (cumin, bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon) and fry until fragrant. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown
  3. Cook Vegetables: Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; sauté for 1 minute. Add mixed vegetables, biryani masala, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes until slightly tender.
  4. Add Yogurt: Reduce heat and stir in the yogurt, cooking for another 2 minutes until the oil separates.
  5. Layer and Cook: Layer the soaked rice over the vegetables. Add 1.5 to 2 cups of water (adjust for desired consistency). Top with mint and coriander leaves.
  6. Dum Cooking: Cover with a tight-fitting lid. Cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is fluffy and fully cooked.
  7. Rest: Turn off the heat and let the biryani rest, covered, for 10 minutes before serving
